About The tail to tale story of us:

Here at The Daily Dawg we believe our four-legged friends deserve to enjoy all that life has to offer. From fashion to tasty treats we want your dog to feel like he's the only one in the world that has it this good when surprised with something new. Look around the store. There are many one-of-a kind items and we always love to hear new ideas and requests to add to our store. Our contact page is ready for your input. Until then...hope you enjoy what we offer here. *10% of total purchase price is donated to the SPCA. Thanks, all!
