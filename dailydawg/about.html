About The Daily Dawg-

Here at The Daily Dawg we love our four-legged friends with everything we have. We believe that they deserve anything and everything to live a full happy life. From Dog toys to unique dog fashion we strive to bring all walks a life something they can enjoy. Take a look around our store and see what may make your little pup the extra bit to have him do "zoomies" or wag a mile a minute! 10% of proceeds are donated to those that have yet to find their forever homes as well, we thank you in advance for your generosity by purchasing through The Daily Dawg.
